SERVICES

What we offer


Sentence Based Analysis

We provide the facility to analysis sentiment of one feedback string in detail. Provide your feedback and check the sentiment associated with the feedback on the go.

Feedback Data Analysis

We provide services to analyse your feedback files stored on your local storage, system or cloud. The all dimension analysis, including 'Aspect Based Analysis', of your document is provided over the training dataset of your selected choice. This makes the service domain-independent.

Cloud Storage

Our cloud plug-ins enable you to store all your feedback data on cloud. In addition, you can provide a URL for files stored at your cloud server, and we will analyse it on real-time.



Web API

We provide facility to generate web API embedded feedback form link. You would have to embed it in your website, and we'll provide you real-time analysis over the feedbacks given through API Link.

Suggestions for Improvement

We enable you to get some of the valuable user feedbacks over defects of the services or products, in which they provide their opinions for improvements. These opinions or suggestions may help a lot in making business decision and analysing user needs.

Proficient Result Page

We facilitate the clients with attractive result pages. The result pages have robust projection along with various filtration options. We also provide suggestions for improvements. Clients can download reports of analysis in various formats as per requirement.
